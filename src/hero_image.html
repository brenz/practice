Our Accreditation

Currently, the School is provisionally approved by the Bureau for Private Postsecondary Education to offer degree programs and has preliminary accreditation from the Liaison Committee of Medical Educations.

As a new medical school, the Kaiser Permanente School of Medicine is in the process of obtaining required accreditation and approval status.
